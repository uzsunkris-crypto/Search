About SEARCH

SEARCH is a Nigeria-first passenger–driver coordination platform built to reduce waiting time and make public transport efficient. It connects passengers and drivers heading the same direction — without private ridesharing. The platform prioritises speed, simplicity, and safety.

How it works

1 — Choose Mode
Pick Passenger or Driver. Switch anytime.
2 — Set pickup & destination
Passengers publish pickup and destination; drivers publish their route.
3 — Connect
Passengers see drivers heading their way; they choose the driver they prefer.
4 — Verify & Rate
Proximity verification ensures authenticity. Passengers rate drivers (stars only).

Our promise

  • No private vehicle registration details shown.
  • Only drivers' first names are visible to passengers.
  • Payments are verified server-side (secure).
  • Map focuses on Nigeria only.
